Terms of Service

TERMS

The client is responsible for providing all the necessary references, descriptions and images, such as poses, expressions, or color palettes.

-You are purchasing the digital file.

-Commercial commissions costs 2x the original price.

-Artwork may be posted on social media and mention the client. Please let me know if you wish to remain anonymous.

-Commissioned pieces should not be altered or edited without the artist's permission.

-The artist has the rights to refuse a commission request.

PAYMENT & REFUNDS

-Payment only through PayPal either in USD or EURO.

-Payment has to be done full upfront in order to start the commission.

-If either the client or the artist cancel the commission before starting working on it, the client will receive a full refund.

-The artist will issue a full refund if they aren't able to complete the commission at any given stage.



-The artist has the rights to refuse a commission request.

-Completed artwork cannot be refunded. If the client requests a refund, these are the following percentages you might receive depending on the progress made:

REFUND PERCENTAGE REFUNDED:

SKETCH PHASE 80% REFUND

LINEART PHASE 40% REFUND

HALFWAY THROUGH COLORING 20% REFUND

CHANGES

-Commissioners can ask for WIPs at any moment. The artist will send them for confirmation or feedback before proceeding to further drawing stages such as inking or color correction.

-Changes can be made during the sketch process, such as adding small details or corrections for free.

-Major changes such as changing clothing, adding other extra elements or changing poses will be charged extra if the drawing is past the ink/coloring stage. Exceptions can be made depending on the degree of difficulty of said changes. Please make sure everything is correctly specified.
